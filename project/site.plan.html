Career Coaching DRC – Site Plan

Prepared by: William Pelo Kibwila

Site Name

Name: Career Coaching DRC

This name reflects a professional platform dedicated to helping students and job seekers in the Democratic Republic of Congo prepare for successful careers. It emphasizes coaching, guidance, and resources for personal and professional growth.

Optional Domain: careercoaching-drc.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a hub for career guidance in the DRC by providing resources such as career advice, job readiness tips, resume and interview guidance, and access to coaching sessions. It will also highlight local opportunities and workshops to help users transition into the workforce successfully.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find tips for creating a professional resume tailored to employers in Congo?
  • How do I connect with a career coach for personalized guidance?
  • What training or workshops are available to prepare me for job opportunities?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue) – used for headings and navigation background.

Secondary Color: #18BC9C (Teal) – used for buttons, accents, and call-to-action highlights.

Neutral Background: #F9F9F9 (Light Gray) – used for page backgrounds.

Dark Blue Teal Light Gray

Typography

Heading Font: Sansita Sans (Google Fonts) – used for titles and headings.

Body Font: Roboto (Google Fonts) – used for paragraphs, forms, and general content.

Special Accent Font: Italic Roboto – used for quotes or emphasis.
